TC1: Verify selling USD by average rate
1. Open https://finance.i.ua/ page
2. Go to Currency converter page
3. Select sell option
4. Select USD currency from currency drop down list
5. Put a sum of USD in a Sum field
6. Select average rate from rates drop down list
7. Verify results:
UAH = sum * avr USD buying rate
EUR = sum * avr USD buying rate / avr EUR selling rate
RUB = sum * avr USD buying rate / avr RUB selling rate
//=============================================================
